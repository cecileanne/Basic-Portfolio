Cecile-Anne Sison (Cecile) is a Canadian living in Chicago with a background in filmmaking, photography, metadata and instructional technology.

She is currently the Instructional Technology Lead of the Media and Design Studio at Northwestern University, where she supports course enhancement for Undergraduate Humanities classes via creating digital projects and deploying digital tools. Her main interests lie in incorporating game design into pedagogy. She also manages a wonderful team that circulates Audio/Visual and various resources to students in these supported courses.

Having lived and worked all over the world in collaborative and creative ventures, she is mindful and open to other points of view and is always eager to learn new things. Other interests include throwing theme parties, Pokemon Go, archery and finding new projects to collaborate on.
